Miikwasouski Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

Miikwasouski Chamber of Commerce. The name reperesents is a fictional city the chamber operates in.

Site Purpose

The Miikwasouski Chamber of Commerce provides a hub for economic growth within the great city of Miikwasouski. We promote local businesses by hosting community events, hiring opportunities, and driving economic development through membership services and pushing for improved public policy.

Scenarios

  • What community events are happening this month?
  • What are the membership benefits?
  • What has been the economic development in this city over the past year?

Color Scheme

The color scheme will center around differing shades of green and blue to represent wealth and security.
